My law career in Russia

My career in law started when I was hired by an attorney of the Law office 'Padva and partners' in April 2001, as an assistant. I've been studying at the 4th course of the university at that moment.

In several years, in 2004, I passed the bar exams and became a licenced attorney.

In all these years I practiced many areas of law, including civil and criminal law. I represented clients in various disputes before courts, mostly on business matters.

A significant place in my practice was occupied by representation of clients before Russian arbitrazh courts on various matters, including bankruptcy, corporate disputes, suits contesting decisions of state bodies, including disputes on state procurement and tax disputes.

I acted for my clients in criminal proceedings on charges of economic crime, and in inspections of business entities performed by state authorities.

At the same time, I represented private individuals in general courts in various civil cases, and provided transaction support, including real estate transactions.

This is a link to my page on the Law office 'Padva and partners' site.

As I moved to Israel in 2022, my bar licence is paused since then.
